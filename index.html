About this game

Letâ€™s run is a single player fun digital game for all ages that allows a player to run endlessly until the course overcomes the player's hand and eye coordination. The game play will consist of an endless trail that will have obstacles and missing parts of the platform, which could either hurt the player or kill them. With this game, reflexes is the key, as the further the player progresses on the trail the harder the game will become. Obstacles such as spikes will appear frequently, platforms will appear at random distances and elevations, while the game play speed will increase. Despite its challenge, the game offers players the convenience to play anywhere in the world and compare scores among other players.

Throughout the game, the player will encounter this symbol. This will allow the player to receive double points and no spikes for a limited time.
